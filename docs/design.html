Design page

Within this page are a variety of websites which have been beneficial in the design process for this website.

Apple

Apple has always been a key innovator when it comes to technology, so having a responsive website would be imperitive to their success. Having analysed their site by looking at how they layout their pages and their navigation; for my site I have taken inspiration from their large use of pictures to direct the user through the page. Large displays of products and colours helps to keep users engaged and is a clever way to split the website page up. How I have managed to implement this into my page is to introduce each page with a large splash image which is full of colour or relevant to the page. By having pictures throughout the page as well; this was used as another method to help with the layout of the site. Take a look at Apple's UK site for further examples and techniques, please visit here.

D3R

Codecademy is an online freemium interactive platform that offers free coding classes in 12 different programming languages including Python, Java, JavaScript (jQuery, AngularJS, React.js), Ruby, SQL, and Sass, as well as markup languages HTML and CSS. The site also offers a paid "pro" option that gives users access to a personalized learning plan, quizzes, realistic projects, and live help from advisors. The platform also provides courses for learning command line and Git. This is a great resource if you wanted to pursue a career in coding and wanted to begin to learn the fundamental skills to start your journey. Zurb

Google Design is a cooperative effort led by a group of designers, writers, and developers at Google. They work across teams to publish original content, produce events, and foster creative and educational partnerships that advance design and technology. Googles Material design podcasts and content are amazing tools to help with your understanding of new design trends and to help you stay one step ahead. Complete with material design guidelines, components, color tools, icons and galleries it is a great resource to take advantage of.
