Lastly a word from our founder:

I would like to say as a start, that I had found it quite enjoyable to get to grips with the mark-up languages of HTML and CSS and to really test my knowledge when trying to apply them outside the classroom.

Being able to get creative and learn essentially what is a new language; has been challenging in some ways but mostly exciting to get to build a website within weeks of starting the course.

Learning all about material design has also been beneficial to help understand the trending layouts of websites and to apply this to our own sites.

To add, being taught the importance of how icons, API's and favicons all add to the design of the website and how to take a site into a more professional standing.

Our tutor Liz has been amazing from the outset and hopefully gives me a good grade (just kidding!) but her video's and material have been great to use and to apply to our own sites.

Last of all, I believe that as I begin to learn more about web development I can truely say that a career in this field is a high possibility due to how I have enjoyed this term.

- Elliott Garner
